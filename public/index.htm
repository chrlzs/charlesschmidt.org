Charles Schmidt - full-stack technology leader

About

Charlie Schmidt is a full-stack technology leader and professional with over 25 years of experience working with clients across a breadth of industries including fintech, health care, consumer goods, agriculture and food service and so many more.

Whether it's scrum planning, estimating project life cycles, architecture and technical design, website development, ecommerce, iOS or Android development, code reviews, or mentoring tech staff, Charlie's deep experience in the tech space allows him to seamlessly integrate with and provide measurable value to tech teams and companies.

Charlie is an avid believer in ongoing education to stay on top of an ever-changing tech landscape as displayed by his many certifications and certificates. He is currently pursuing a cybersecurity degree to further build on the value of services he provides.

When not at work, Charlie enjoys exploring home automation, taking German lessons, and spending time with his wife, two children and dogs.
